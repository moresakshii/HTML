About meesho

Meesho is one of India's leading e-commerce marketplaces, founded in 2015 by Vidit Aatrey and Sanjeev Barnwal. Headquartered in Bengaluru, Meesho aims to democratize internet commerce by making online buying and selling accessible to everyone across India.
The platform connects millions of customers with sellers, entrepreneurs, and small businesses, offering a wide range of products including fashion, home essentials, beauty products, electronics accessories, and daily-use items. Meesho is known for its affordable pricing and focus on serving customers in both urban and smaller towns across India.
