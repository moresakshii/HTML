Artificial Intelligence (AI) uses computer systems to simulate human intelligence. By analyzing vast amounts of data, AI algorithms can learn, reason, solve complex problems, and make decisions autonomously. Today, it powers everyday technologies like virtual assistants, predictive medical tools, and self-driving cars, rapidly transforming how humanity lives and works.

Computer science is the study of computers and computational systems, focusing on software, algorithms, and data processing. It combines theoretical mathematics with practical engineering to design efficient programs, manage complex databases, and secure digital networks.As technology evolves, computer science drives innovation in automation, artificial intelligence, and everyday digital tools
